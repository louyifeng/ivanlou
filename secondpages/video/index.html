PROJECT: TYPE IN MOTION

Type in Motion
Motion Graphic | 2017 | College Project

 

"I have not failed. I've just found 10,000 ways that won't work"

This is a collaborative project with Janice Salikin. With 200 light bulbs, we created this video.

This quote by Thomas Edison embraced the idea of failure. We need to experience different ways of failure to success. The video is a representation of his journey to success.
