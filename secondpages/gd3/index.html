PROJECT: LIFE ON THE MOVE

LIFE ON THE MOVE
ANIMAL MIGRATION PROTECTION
System Design | 2017 | College Project

 

Life on the Move is an organization that protects animal migration. People should be aware of how human development is effecting animal migrations and we should find ways to continue living together with animals.

In this project, I try to not use any images of animals. Instead, I am using dots to represent all kinds of animals and to show the movement of animal migration. Based on this, I also developed patterns for this system.

The mission statement: It’s so good to be home. Animals should feel the same.

Lecture

On the front of the cards, I have the analysis on the water (their source, taste, PH, and final score). I also rename the water based on their taste. On the back of the cards, I reveal where the water is from.
