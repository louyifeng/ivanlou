Dangerous Ideas on Design Education
The book is based on a manifesto written by James Victore. My main focus is on the humanism shown in this article about how we should embrace our own weirdness and characteristics. Thus, my concept is about the imperfections of human such as asymmetric birthmark on left and right hands and the imperfections made by human such as a chewed up straw.
