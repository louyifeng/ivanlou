Ivan Lou 楼逸峰
Hello, I am Ivan Lou, a student at California College of the Arts in San Francisco. I was born and raised in Hangzhou, home of the world’s best green tea. I love design that people interact with because it allows the design to become a part of the users. Design with motion also interests me because motion brings flat graphics to a next level. I love design because it gives me a sense of existence. It is a rewarding experience to see my design being online or in shops. I hope my design can be a part of people’s life and be memorized. When I am not working, you will find me watching TV dramas with a cup of soy milk or making over-sized T-shirts.
