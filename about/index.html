Ivan Lou 楼逸峰
Hello, I am Ivan Lou, a student at California College of the Arts in San Francisco. I was born and raised in Hangzhou, home of the world’s best green tea.

I love traditional graphic design including posters, books and logos. I particularly enjoy designing with motion and interaction. I believe this makes design more visually appealing and brings it to a next level. I enjoy when people are inspired by my work and become curious about my work process. Having my work existing in the world matters a lot to me, and the best way to achieve it is for people to remember my work. It gives me a sense of achievement when people are surprised by what I can do with design. I love graphic design also because it allows me to work with different industries and explore the different possibility I can do with design for a variety of people.

When I am not working, you will find me watching TV dramas with a cup of soy milk or making over-sized T-shirts.
