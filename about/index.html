Ivan Lou 楼逸峰
Hello, I am Ivan Lou, a current student at California College of the Arts in San Francisco. I was born and raised in Hangzhou where the best green is. I love design that moves and interacts with people because it allows the design to become a part of the audience. When I am not working, you will find me watching drama with a cup of soy milk or making over-sized T-shirts. I love design because it gives me a sense of existence. I hope my design can influence people’s life and change how people think.
